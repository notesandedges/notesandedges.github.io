Shivani Shukla is a graduate student at the University of California, San Diego in the Department of Bioengineering. She is a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow. Her interests lie at the intersection of nanotechnology and neuroscience, specifically in creating electrical recording technologies for the brain. She has conducted research in labs across the world, including at Nagoya University in Japan, at the Technion in Israel, and at Carnegie Mellon. She hopes to become a leader in neuroengineering and develop affordable technologies for low-resourced communities around the globe.
